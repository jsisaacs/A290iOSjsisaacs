Overview:
Logs cumulative data regarding tax deductions and then exports it to a 
formatted pdf that contains all relavent info in a year end summary (category
(professional memberships, automobile expenses, travel/meetings, office staff,
etc.), items in each category, total $ per category, and then a grand total
for year) that users can give to employers, tax papers, etc.
